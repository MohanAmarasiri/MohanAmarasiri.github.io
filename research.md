<div class="research-intro">
  My research focuses on the human-environment interface, specifically within the realm of environmental engineering. I aim to improve urban life by evaluating how environmental contaminants impact human health and developing robust strategies to mitigate these risks.
</div>

<div class="research-grid">

  <div class="research-card">
    <a href="/research/pathogen-surveillance/" class="card-title">Pathogen Surveillance & Wastewater Epidemiology</a>
    <p class="card-text">
      Developing advanced methods for tracking viruses (such as Norovirus and SARS-CoV-2) and bacterial pathogens in the environment. This includes "sparse sampling" techniques and utilizing wastewater/environmental matrices as a diagnostic tool for community health.
    </p>
    <span class="research-tags">Viruses • Wastewater • Public Health</span>
  </div>

  <div class="research-card">
    <a href="/research/amr/" class="card-title">Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)</a>
    <p class="card-text">
      Investigating the transport and persistence of antibiotic-resistant bacteria (ARB) and antibiotic resistance genes (ARG) in aquatic environments. My work evaluates how treatment processes and environmental factors influence the spread of resistance.
    </p>
    <span class="research-tags">AMR • ARB/ARG • Water Quality</span>
  </div>

  <div class="research-card">
    <a href="/research/pfas/" class="card-title">Emerging Contaminants (PFAS)</a>
    <p class="card-text">
      Assessing the occurrence and health risks associated with emerging contaminants like PFAS. I focus on understanding their movement through the human-environment interface and identifying effective mitigation strategies.
    </p>
    <span class="research-tags">PFAS • Chemical Safety • Risk Assessment</span>
  </div>

  <div class="research-card">
    <a href="/research/modeling/" class="card-title">Environmental Modeling & Validation</a>
    <p class="card-text">
      Integrating mathematical modeling with experimental validation to predict the status of aquatic environments. This approach allows for the simulation of contaminant behavior and the assessment of potential intervention strategies.
    </p>
    <span class="research-tags">Mathematical Modeling • Experimental Design</span>
  </div>
